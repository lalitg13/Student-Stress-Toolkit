Quick coping strategies

🧊

5-4-3-2-1 Grounding

Name 5 things you see, 4 you can touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste.

🚶

Movement Break

Stand up and take a 5-minute walk, even just around your room. Movement clears your head.

📵

Phone-Free 10 Min

Put your phone face-down for 10 minutes and focus on just one task without notifications.

💧

Drink Water

Dehydration worsens anxiety and brain fog. Drink a full glass of water right now.

✍️

Brain Dump

Write every stressful thought on paper for 2 minutes. Getting it out of your head helps.

🌤️

One Small Win

Pick the smallest possible task you can complete right now. Done beats perfect.
